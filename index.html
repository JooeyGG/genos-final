Appropriate Uses:

Genos is a versatile font suitable for various design applications, especially those with a modern or futuristic aesthetic. Its fourteen variations from Thin to Black offer adaptability, making it suitable for a wide range of designs. With its modern and sleek feel, it's ideal for futuristic, industrial, technical, or contemporary projects. Its diverse styles can be employed for headlines, logos, packaging, and other design elements.

Designer Information:

Rob Leuschke, the founder and CEO of TypeSETit, is a talented graphic designer, lettering artist, and type designer located near St. Louis, Missouri, USA. Over the past four decades, Rob has been dedicated to crafting lettering and graphics for a multitude of uses, including packaging, logos, and social expression products. His expertise in typography and lettering has resulted in the creation of various typefaces, including the Genos font family.

Character Sets and Language Support:

Genos boasts Latin character sets, encompassing Western, Central, and Vietnamese language support. This extensive language coverage allows for the font's use in a broader range of applications and multilingual designs. The inclusion of these language sets enhances the font's versatility and accessibility for global design needs.
